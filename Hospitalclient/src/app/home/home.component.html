Our ClinicalServices

As a multi-speciality hospital based in Chennai, our pre-paid packages make quality health care affordable at equitable costs for the residents of Chennai and beyond. Our goal is to provide innovative and integrated health care to all. It is a privilege for us to treat those who entrust their health to us, and we value the trust and repay the multitude by going above and beyond the expectations in serving them. We are particular in keeping our hospital clean and safe and ensuring that we deliver safe and excellent health care. We are honored to serve every patient who stays with us are our guests. We treat our guests with great respect and courtesy.




Our Mission

“To provide holistic comprehensive healthcare under one roof at an affordable cost.”

Our Vision

“Raising the standards of healthcare and make it Affordable, Equitable and Free-at-The-point of Delivery.”

Our Objectives

The most important objective for which our hospital will be to provide high-quality service at an affordable cost not only for the affluent sections of the society but also to those who may not be economically well off. The outpatient services will be provided on the concept of polyclinics and referral clinics. The outpatient services will be provided not only in the morning hours but also through special clinics in the afternoon and in the evening also at night which mentions as a 24x7 hospital. It would be the endeavor of the hospital to treat patients through ambulatory care as far as possible. Those who need admission will first be fully investigated wherever possible through the diagnostic departments.
